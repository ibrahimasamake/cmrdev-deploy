The International Conference on Intelligent Computing, Information and Communication Technologies (ICIICT)

Theme: “Intelligent Computing for Smart and Connecting World”

All papers should be original, unpublished, and not submitted to any other journals or conferences.

All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism, and papers not satisfying the criteria will be summarily rejected.

All papers must be submitted in Microsoft Word Document format.

Notification of acceptance will be communicated through email.

Registration will be confirmed only after payment of the registration fee, and at least one author must be present at the conference.

All accepted and registered papers will be published in the International Conference Proceedings with ISBN numbers.

Selected papers will be published in UGC Care / Scopus / Web of Science Journals (additional charges apply).
