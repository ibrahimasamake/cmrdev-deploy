About the Conference

About ICIICT

The International Conference on Intelligent Computing, Information and Communication Technologies (ICIICT) is a premier international forum dedicated to fostering research excellence, innovation, and knowledge exchange in emerging areas of computing and communication technologies. The conference brings together academicians, researchers, scientists, industry professionals, and research scholars from across the globe to discuss recent advancements and future directions in the digital landscape. ICIICT covers a broad spectrum of research domains including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Big Data Analytics, Internet of Things, Cloud and Edge Computing, Cyber Security, Blockchain Technologies, and advanced Communication Systems. The conference encourages interdisciplinary and application-oriented research addressing real-world and societal challenges. The conference features keynote addresses by eminent experts, invited talks, panel discussions, and technical paper presentation sessions that facilitate in-depth scholarly interactions. All submitted papers undergo a rigorous peer-review process to ensure quality, originality, and academic relevance. ICIICT also provides a vibrant platform for young researchers and innovators to present their work, receive expert feedback, and establish professional networks. The conference promotes strong academia–industry collaboration and supports the translation of research outcomes into practical applications. The conference is organized by the School of Science and Computer Studies (SSCS) in collaboration with the Directorate of Research and Innovation (DORI), CMR University, Bengaluru, India, underscoring the institution’s commitment to research-driven innovation, academic excellence, and global engagement.

About CMR University

MR University, Bengaluru, is a multidisciplinary private institution established under the CMR University Act, 2013, with a clear focus on delivering quality higher education through innovation and research. The university offers diverse undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes across disciplines such as engineering, management, law, sciences, humanities, economics, education, and design. Its curriculum is designed to be outcome-oriented and industry-relevant, emphasizing experiential learning, analytical thinking, and practical application of knowledge. CMR University encourages interdisciplinary learning through flexible academic structures and choice-based education. Research and innovation are actively promoted through the Directorate of Research and Innovation (DORI), supported by research centers, funded projects, and collaborations at national and international levels. Strong industry partnerships enhance student exposure through internships, projects, and skill development initiatives. The university also supports entrepreneurship through incubation and startup facilitation. Global engagement is strengthened through academic partnerships and exchange programmes. Modern infrastructure and technology-enabled classrooms create a dynamic learning environment. Accredited with an ‘A’ Grade by NAAC, CMR University, Bengaluru, demonstrates its commitment to academic quality, institutional excellence, and continuous improvement.

School of Science and Computer Studies (SSCS)

he School of Science and Computer Studies (SSCS), CMR University, Bengaluru, is a vibrant academic unit committed to delivering high-quality education and research in the fields of science and computing. The school offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes in areas such as Computer Science, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and related disciplines. SSCS adopts a learner-centric and practice-oriented approach that integrates strong theoretical foundations with hands-on laboratory work and real-time projects. The curriculum is regularly updated to align with industry needs and emerging technologies. The school promotes interdisciplinary learning and collaborative research culture among faculty and students. Experienced and qualified faculty members actively contribute through teaching, research publications, and academic innovations. SSCS encourages research activities, funded projects, and participation in conferences and technical forums. Strong industry interactions support internships, skill development, and career readiness. The school also nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities among students. Overall, SSCS plays a significant role in strengthening the academic and research ecosystem of CMR University. The Directorate of Research and Innovation (DORI), CMR University, Bengaluru, is the central hub for promoting research and innovation across the university. It encourages interdisciplinary and collaborative research among faculty, students, and scholars. DORI supports funded projects, publications, patents, and intellectual property development. The directorate fosters industry-academia collaboration and practical application of research outcomes. It also promotes entrepreneurship through incubation, startups, and technology transfer initiatives. Overall, DORI strengthens the university’s research culture and drives innovation excellence.
