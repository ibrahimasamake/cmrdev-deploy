About the Conference

The International Conference on Intelligent Computing, Information and Communication Technologies (ICIICT) is a premier international forum dedicated to fostering research excellence, innovation, and knowledge exchange in emerging areas of computing and communication technologies. The conference brings together academicians, researchers, scientists, industry professionals, and research scholars from across the globe to discuss recent advancements and future directions in the digital landscape.

About ICCIMR

ICCIMR aims to unite innovation, technology, and research by merging cutting-edge technologies with computational intelligence. It facilitates global collaboration among academia and industry.

About CMR University (CMRU)

CMR University (CMRU), Bangalore is a private university established under Act 45 of 2013, fostering innovation, discovery, and interdisciplinary education.

About Directorate of Research & Innovation (DORI)

The Directorate of Research & Innovation (DORI) promotes high-quality research, publications, patents, ethical practices, and global academic engagement.

About School of Science Studies (SOSS)

School of Science Studies (SOSS) focuses on collaborative learning, industry-ready programs, and hands-on professional education.
